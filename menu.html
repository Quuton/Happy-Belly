A colorful trip in Vietnamese cuisine

Cơm Tấm - Vietnamese Broken Rice Meal

This Vietnamese dish is one of the staple foods eaten by the Vietnamese people. It is a plate of rice accompanied by a sweet grilled chop of pork and a meaty egg pie, it is eaten together with a side of vegtables as well as the special sauce.

Hu tieu noodles

Firmly cooked noodles paired with massive amounts of toppings you'll never feel tired of eating it is what Hu Tieu is. Hu Tieu is also paired up with savoury soup to wash down every bite you take.

Bun Thit Noodles

Crave for a variety experience offered from Hu Tieu? Bun thit noodles uses vercimilli rice noodles topped with many toppings in a similar fashion to the Hu Tieu.

But instead of sweet pork char siu, or quail eggs, Bun Thit delivers Grilled pork, together with authentic fried Vietnamese springrolls, the special vietnamese sauce used in broken rice and a side of vegtables.

Fresh spring rolls

Perhaps our best seller, our Vietnamese springrolls use only fresh and healthy ingredients packed into one finger sized springroll, accompanied with our very sweet and savoury peanut sauce.

The "e" in Indonesia stands for energetic

Fried Tumeric chicken

Coming in from Indonesia, is our Turmeric fried chicken, and it isn't your average fried chicken. Our fried chicken is packed with energizing and refreshing Turmeric, it's the ingredient that gives the chicken its golden glow.

Essence of hospitality in Filipino comfort food

Longganisa

Another Filipino favourite, good ol' Longganisa, a sausage that is typically packed with sweet and savoury flavours, making the perfect breakfast component, or your emergency comfort food.

We offer Longganisa as a frozen package, cooked, or prepared in a full meal with rice and a sunny side up egg.
